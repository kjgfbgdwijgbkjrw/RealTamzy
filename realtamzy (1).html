The Origin

WHO IS
TAMZY

It started with a simple idea: react to every Kanye West album. What began as a passion project became the fuel that kept Tamzy streaming — building a loyal fanbase of Kanye fans who showed up for every drop, every reaction, every moment.

Born 9th November 2005, Tamzy brings raw energy and genuine love to every stream. Whether it's breaking down the layers of Late Registration or going bar-for-bar on Graduation, the authenticity is what keeps people coming back.

Before the Kanye era, there was FIFA — hours of it — which led to connections with niche football content creators. That crossover audience adds a unique dimension to the Tamzy universe. Short-form clips on TikTok and Reels keep things alive between streams, mixing Kanye-coded humour with everyday funny moments.
